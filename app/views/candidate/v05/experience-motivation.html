Experience and motivation

What motivates you to join the profession and why do you think you have potential as a teacher?
Bear in mind that you don't have to teach the subject of your degree. You can also complete a course to teach a subject you studied at A level, as a module of your degree, or that you have expertise in through work. Talk to your training provider, or learn more at Get into Teaching.
I don’t have any school experience
You can still submit an application without school experience but a few days observing in a school will help you decide if teaching is for you. Find out more.
Tell us about your experiences working with children or in a school. Include voluntary and paid work and give us details of your role, responsibilities and length of involvement. You can include as many different types of work or experience as you like, including observation in a school.
You may wish to mention a disability, special need or other health or personal issue you feel is relevant to your application. Your training provider will use information about disability to make sure their interview days are accessible.
